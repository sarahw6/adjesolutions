Doula Services

Celebrating the miracle of life

First off, congratulations to you! If you are on this page then most likely you are expecting, and I am so excited for you! I am a mother of 5 children, I delivered them all naturally in different African countries. I LOVED the birthing experience and that is why I decided to become a doula so I could share my joy with you!

I bring good news, YOU ARE MADE FOR THIS!!! And, your body already knows what to do, so sit back and watch, feel, enjoy the miracle of life that you are about to witness.

I am based internationally so while I cannot be physically at your bedside when you deliver (assuming you are delivering in the United States or elsewhere) I can still coach you throughout your pregnancy to prepare for the big day. I help prepare you mentally for the delivery itself and practically on the logistics involved in welcoming a new baby into your home. I can join you virtually for a part of your birth and/or link you to my network of stateside doulas if you are delivering in the US.

Let's have a call to talk about babies, one of my all time favorite topics!

What I Offer

Mental Preparation for Delivery

Build confidence and mental readiness for the birthing experience. Learn techniques to stay calm, focused, and empowered throughout labor and delivery.

Practical Logistics Coaching

Get organized and ready for your new arrival. From hospital bag essentials to home preparation, I help you plan the practical details of welcoming a new baby.

Virtual Birth Support

I can join you virtually during part of your birth to provide encouragement, guidance, and emotional support in real time, no matter where you are in the world.

Stateside Doula Network

Delivering in the US? I can connect you with my trusted network of stateside doulas for in-person support during your birth experience.

Let's Talk About Babies!

I would love to hear from you and support you on this incredible journey. Let's chat about one of my all time favorite topics!
