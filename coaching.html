1:1 Leadership Coaching

Your personal champion in the ringside corner

Are you stressed and overwhelmed in your job and wondering how to create the work-life balance you need? Are you navigating a career transition and would like more support? Are you facing a difficult situation in the workplace with a supervisor, subordinate, or peer, and need help finding a solution? Do you feel like an underdog in your workplace and need someone in your corner to help you strategize your rise up?

I've been there. With nearly two decades of international experience rising up the ladder I have seen it all. Let me help you discover a better way to work and to get ahead that will bring you peace. I authored DIPLOMATICALLY: A Guide for Creating Work-life Balance in the Foreign Service (2024 Amazon bestseller and ranked #2 on BookAuthority) to share my 3Cs approach™ (Connection, Communication, and Courage) that will help you reach your personal goals in the workplace. I will show you how to build deeper relationships, fine tune your communication style, and show up more intentionally to achieve your objectives. I start my coaching from where you are at and serve as your loyal thought partner and trusted advisor from day one. I am in your ringside corner coaching you to win the championship. I offer one on one coaching packages tailored to your needs.

The 3Cs Approach™

A proven framework for professional growth and workplace success

Connection

Build deeper, more meaningful professional relationships that create trust, open doors, and strengthen your influence across teams and organizations.

Communication

Fine-tune your communication style for maximum impact. Learn to navigate difficult conversations, advocate for yourself, and convey your ideas with clarity and confidence.

Courage

Show up intentionally and achieve your objectives with confidence. Develop the mindset and strategies to take bold steps in your career, even when the path feels uncertain.
